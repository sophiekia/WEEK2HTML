What is a Domestic Rabbit?

Rabbits belong to the mammal family named Leporidae. The europeon, or old world rabbit (Oryctolagus cuniculus; of Europe and Asia) lives in warrens consisting of burrows; this species is the ancestor to all domestic breeds. They are a subspecies of European rabbit and member of the lagomorph family
